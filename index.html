After tracking hundreds of days, these were the real predictors of my morning stability:

The Evening Timeline That Controls the Morning (for me)

  • Last liquid intake: stop roughly 6 PM, absolute cutoff around 9:40 PM

  • Sleep start time: consistent window between midnight and 1 AM
  • Minimum sleep: 7 hours, ideally stable

When these line up, my mornings are predictable.
When they don't, things go sideways.
