Why timing matters

Many people misinterpret a biological crash as a mental problem. In reality, the gut-brain axis reacts strongly to carb load, sugar spikes, caffeine timing, and gut nerve sensitivity. When blood sugar drops, or when the gut is irritated, the nervous system often fires a “threat” signal — which feels just like anxiety.
